    The Virginia Tech Hokies are the athletic teams officially representing the Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University in intercollegiate athletics. The Hokies participate in the NCAA’s Division I Atlantic Coast Conference in 22 varsity sports. Virginia Tech’s men’s sports are football, basketball, baseball, cross country, golf, soccer, swimming and diving, tennis, indoor and outdoor track and field, and wrestling. Virginia Tech’s women’s sports are basketball, cross country, lacrosse, soccer, softball, swimming and diving, tennis, indoor and outdoor track and field, golf, and volleyball. Athletes representing the Hokies have won national titles in various track and field events and in wrestling. Though not affiliated with the NCAA, Virginia Tech won the 2007 national championship of bass fishing. The Hokie men’s basketball team won the 1973 and 1995 NIT tournaments and went to the Sweet Sixteen of “March Madness” in 1967 and 2019.
