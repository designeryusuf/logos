    The Bloomsburg Huskies are the athletic teams that represent Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania, located in Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, in NCAA Division II intercollegiate sports. The Huskies are members of the Pennsylvania State Athletic Conference for 18 of 19 varsity sports; as the wrestling team competes in the Mid-American Conference as a member of the NCAA’s Division I. The Huskies have been a member of the PSAC since its foundation in 1951.
