    Kyoto Sanga FC is a Japanese professional football club based in Kyoto. “Sanga” comes from the Sanskrit sangha, a term meaning “group” or “club” and often used to denote the Buddhist priesthood, associating the club with Kyoto’s many Buddhist temples. The club was formerly known as Kyoto Purple Sanga with “purple”, the colour of the team uniforms, an imperial colour reflecting Kyoto’s status as Japan’s ancient imperial capital city. It was decided however that, from 2007, the team will simply been known as “Kyoto Sanga”. They are the oldest club competing in the J.League.
