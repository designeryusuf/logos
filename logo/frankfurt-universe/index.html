    The Frankfurt Universe are a German American football team from Frankfurt, Hesse. The club, formed in 2007, won promotion to the German Football League 2, the second tier of league football in Germany, in 2011. The team rebranded itself as “Frankfurt Galaxy” ahead of the 2015 season and won promotion to the first division German Football League Süd at the end of this season. It was renamed again as Frankfurt Universe in 2016.

    Logos fall into three classifications (which can be combined).
