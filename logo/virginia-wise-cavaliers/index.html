    The Virginia–Wise Cavaliers, nicknamed the “Highland Cavaliers” before 2017, are the athletic teams that represent the University of Virginia’s College at Wise, located in Wise, Virginia, in NCAA Division II intercollegiate sports. The Cavaliers compete as members of the South Atlantic Conference for all 13 varsity sports, having joined that league from the Mountain East Conference in 2019. UVA Wise has also been a past member of the Great Midwest Athletic Conference.
