    The Georgia State Panthers represent the NCAA Division I sports teams of Georgia State University. GSU’s teams (except beach volleyball, which competes in the Coastal Collegiate Sports Association) are members of the Sun Belt Conference, a conference of which they were a charter member. Previously, GSU was a member of the CAA, and prior to that, the Atlantic Sun Conference (then known as the Trans America Athletic Conference, or TAAC).
