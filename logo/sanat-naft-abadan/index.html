    Sanat Naft Abadan Football Club is an Iranian football club based in Abadan, Iran. They currently play in the Persian Gulf Pro League after being promoted from the Azadegan League in the 2015–2016 season. The team is sponsored by Iran’s national petroleum industry and is part of the sports club Sanat Naft Athletic Club. Sanat Naft has a reserve team, Sanat Naft Novin who currently compete in the 2nd Division. Despite their lack of success in past years, they are still heavily supported by the people of Abadan, and have cemented a place in Iran’s football history.
