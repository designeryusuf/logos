    About AT&T company

    AT&T Inc. began its history as Southwestern Bell Telephone Company, which was a subsidiary of the Bell Telephone Company founded by Alexander Graham Bell in 1880. Bell Telephone Company evolved into American Telephone and Telegraph Company in 1885 which had since rebranded to AT&T Corporation. Now AT&T Inc. is an American multinational conglomerate holding company, headquartered at Whitacre Tower in downtown Dallas, Texas. AT&T is the world’s largest telecommunications company.
