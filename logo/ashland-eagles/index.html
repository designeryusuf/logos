    The Ashland Eagles are the athletic teams that represent Ashland University, located in Ashland, Ohio, in NCAA Division II intercollegiate sports. The Eagles are members of the Great Lakes Intercollegiate Athletic Conference for 21 of their 22 varsity sports. The Eagles have been members of the GLIAC since 1995. They’ll be joining the Great Midwest Athletic Conference in 2021.
