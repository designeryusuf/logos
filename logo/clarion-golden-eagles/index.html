    The Clarion Golden Eagles are the athletic teams that represent the Clarion University of Pennsylvania, located in Clarion, Pennsylvania, in NCAA Division II intercollegiate sports. The Golden Eagles are members of the Pennsylvania State Athletic Conference for 13 of 14 varsity sports; the wrestling team competes in the Eastern Wrestling League as a member of the NCAA’s Division I. The Golden Eagles have been a member of the PSAC since its foundation in 1951.
