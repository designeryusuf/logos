    Αnorthosis Famagusta, known as Anorthosis, is the football team of the Cypriot sports club of the same name. Originally located in Famagusta, the club has been based in Larnaca since 1974 due to the Turkish invasion of Cyprus. Anorthosis was founded in 1911 in Famagusta and in 1934 became one of the founder clubs of the Cyprus Football Association. Their home ground is the Antonis Papadopoulos Stadium in Larnaca, and the president of the club is Evgenios Chamboullas. One of the most successful clubs in Cypriot football, Anorthosis has won the Cypriot First Division 13 times, the Cypriot Cup 11 times and the Super Cup 7 times. Anorthosis is one of three Cypriot clubs never to have been relegated to the second division, was the first Cypriot club to participate in the Champions League group stage, and the first Cypriot club to qualify for the Europa Conference League group stage.
