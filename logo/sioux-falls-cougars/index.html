    The Sioux Falls Cougars are the athletic teams that represent the University of Sioux Falls. They currently compete in NCAA Division II after participating in the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics through 2011. They were formally members of the Great Plains Athletic Conference from 2000 to 2011 and the South Dakota Interstate Conference until the year 2000. The university is a member of the Northern Sun Intercollegiate Conference.
