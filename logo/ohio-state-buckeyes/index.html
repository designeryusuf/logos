    The Ohio State Buckeyes are the intercollegiate athletic teams that represent Ohio State University, located in Columbus, Ohio. The athletic programs are named after the colloquial term for people from the state of Ohio and after the state tree, the Ohio Buckeye. The Buckeyes participate in the National Collegiate Athletic Association’s Division I in all sports and the Big Ten Conference in most sports. The Ohio State women’s ice hockey team competes in the Western Collegiate Hockey Association. The school colors are scarlet and gray. The university’s mascot is Brutus Buckeye. Ohio State is one of only six universities to have won an NCAA national championship in baseball and men’s basketball, and be recognized as a national champion in football.
