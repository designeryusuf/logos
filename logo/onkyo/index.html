    Onkyo Corporation is a Japanese consumer electronics manufacturer, specializing in premium home cinema and audio equipment, including AV receivers, surround sound speakers and portable devices. The word Onkyo translates as “sound resonance”. On is from Chinese pronunciation, with traditional Japanese pronunciation as Oto, meaning “the sound”. Kyo is also from Chinese pronunciation, pronounced as Hibiki or Hibiku in traditional Japanese, meaning “resound, sound, or echo”. The company started under the name of Osaka Denki Onkyo K.K. in 1946. The current Onkyo Corporation umbrella includes the Integra and Integra Research divisions as well as the main Onkyo brand. In March 2015, Onkyo purchased Pioneer Corporation’s Home Electronics Corporation, which produces home cinema amplifiers, Blu-ray players and other AV products. In return, Pioneer took a 14.95% stake in Onkyo.
