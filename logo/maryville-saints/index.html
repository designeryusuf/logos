    The Maryville Saints are the athletic teams that represent Maryville University of St. Louis, located in St. Louis, Missouri, in NCAA Division II intercollegiate sports. The Saints compete as members of the West Division of the Great Lakes Valley Conference for all 23 varsity sports. Maryville has been a full member of the GLVC since 2009 and became an active member of Division II in July 2011. The Saints had formerly competed in the St. Louis Intercollegiate Athletic Conference, a Division III conference, since 1989 and had competed in Division III sports since 1978.
