    The Utah Valley University Wolverines represent Utah Valley University in NCAA DI collegiate athletics and sponsor 16 sporting programs. The Wolverines participate in the Western Athletic Conference. The school mascot is the Wolverine, and the colors are green and white. The UVU student section is called The Den. A name change from The Mawl, the student section’s previous name, was enacted summer 2017. All UVU students can get their free Den Pass, which includes free admission to all athletic events.
