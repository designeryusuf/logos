    The St. Cloud State Huskies are the athletic teams for St. Cloud State University. The university is primarily a member of the Northern Sun Intercollegiate Conference, and currently sponsors 18 NCAA Division II teams. SCSU also sponsors a women’s Nordic skiing team through membership in the CCSA, as well as men’s and women’s Division I ice hockey teams that are members of the National Collegiate Hockey Conference and Western Collegiate Hockey Association. The teams go by the nickname Huskies, and the school’s mascot is a husky named Blizzard. The Director of Athletics is currently Heather Weems.
